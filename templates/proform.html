American Plate

A Trend Analysis of Agriculture and Livestock Products

American Plate is a web-based user interface that allows you to compare food production with pricing across the United States. Discover new trends from the stored data in relation to agricultural and meat products by using one of our complex queries! This is an amazing tool that can be used by local consumers, farmers, companies, and legislators to better understand food production and its economic impact on society. Using data from the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) and pulling over 450,000 tuples, our goal is to discover unique trends as the data changes over time.
